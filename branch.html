Branches of Marine Biology

Marine Ecology

Explore the interactions between marine organisms and their environment, including ecosystems like coral reefs and mangroves.

Marine Biotechnology

Learn about the use of marine resources for developing new medicines, materials, and technologies.

Marine Mammology

Focus on protecting marine species and habitats, addressing issues like pollution and overfishing.

Biological Oceanography

Study the physical and chemical properties of the ocean, including currents, waves, and the ocean floor.

Marine phycology

Dive into the study of marine animals, from tiny plankton to massive whales.
